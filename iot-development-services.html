Consumer IoT

Clinical Care

We develop connected diagnostics tools, medication dispensers, and IoT-enabled patient care systems to support hospitals and clinics. Our solutions help reduce readmissions, enable continuous care, and ensure accurate data flow between patients and providers.

Our Approach to IoT Application Development

01

Connect

At Manektech, we bring everyday objects to life by enabling them to communicate and share real-time data through internet gateways. We make this possible using advanced communication interfaces and networking protocols like NFC, Wi-Fi, BLE, along with RFID tags, beacons, and smart sensors.

02

Collect

We gather real-time data from connected devices, sensors, and gateways to uncover patterns, detect anomalies, and understand system behaviors. This foundational step enables deeper analysis and paves the way for intelligent automation.

03

Validate

To ensure flawless performance, we build working prototypes that simulate real-world interactions and data flow. This allows us to detect early issues, verify system architecture, and refine the design before full-scale deployment.

04

Visualize

We create intuitive user interfaces and dashboards that transform complex data into actionable insights. Our human-centered design approach ensures every interaction is seamless, efficient, and aligned with user needs.

05

Build

Our expert teams engineer complete IoT ecosystems—blending embedded firmware, APIs, and cloud platforms. From device control to data processing, we deliver secure and scalable solutions tailored to your business.

06

Sustain

We go beyond launch, offering ongoing support to ensure system stability and growth. From deployment to upgrades and remote monitoring, we keep your IoT operations secure, reliable, and optimized for performance.
